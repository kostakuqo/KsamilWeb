Snorkeling

Be Watter

“The territories where you can practice snorkeling are the Ksamil Islands and Tongo Island. Enjoy the snorkeling experience by renting a boat, canoe, or pedal boat. The Boat Trip & BBQ to Tongo Island experience includes snorkeling in the Blue Pool and around Tongo Island. Meanwhile, the Snorkeling & BBQ to Ksamil Islands experience encompasses snorkeling in the Ksamil Islands. During the snorkeling adventure, you will encounter the Ionian meadows, the imperial urchin, red starfish, yellow coral, fish inhabiting the island’s shores, octopus, and pina.”

Ksamil Islands

The islands of Ksamil are four small islands located in the district of Saranda in the south of Albania. The islands can be reached by swimming, canoeing, pedalo or by boat. The islands are part of the Butrint National Park and a protected area which are administered by AKZM (National Agency of Protected Areas) through AdZM Vlorë (Administration of Protected Areas Vlorë). The Ksamil Islands are known for their picturesque landscape surrounded by the crystal Clear waters of the Ionian Sea Sailing to the islands of Ksamil to swim, walk, dive, explore and enjoy the country’s food will sweeten your vacation days
